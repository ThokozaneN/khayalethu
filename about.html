Our Beginning

A store born from
understanding.

Khayalethu Grocery Store was founded in 2018 by members of the community who understood a simple truth: families shouldn't have to choose between paying rent and buying groceries.

We grew up here. We understand the rhythm of life in Embalenhle — the weeks before payday when money is tight, the unexpected expenses that throw budgets off balance, and the pride that comes with providing for your family.

That's why we created a different kind of grocery store. One built on trust, dignity, and the belief that everyone deserves access to essential groceries, regardless of when payday falls.

Our Mission & Values

The principles that guide everything we do.

Our Mission

"To ensure no family in our community goes without essential groceries during tough times. We exist to bridge the gap between need and payday with dignity, trust, and respect."

Trust

We believe in our community. Our credit system is built on mutual trust and respect — no complicated checks, just honest relationships.

Dignity

Needing help isn't something to be ashamed of. We treat every customer with the respect and privacy they deserve.

Community

We're not just a business — we're neighbors. We invest in our community and celebrate its successes.

Transparency

No hidden fees, no complicated terms. What you see is what you get — clear, honest pricing always.
