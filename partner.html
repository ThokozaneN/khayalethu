Partner with Us

Build a stronger township economy, together.

We collaborate with suppliers, farmers, logistics providers, fintechs, and community organisations who believe in fair, dignified access to essential groceries in Embalenhle, Secunda, Evander and nearby areas.

Who we partner with

  • Local farmers and food producers
  • Wholesale and FMCG suppliers
  • Transport and last-mile delivery partners
  • Fintech, payment and credit innovators
  • NGOs and community-based organisations

Why partner with Khayalethu?

  • Deep roots in Embalenhle and surrounding communities.
  • Growing base of loyal customers who buy on trust-based credit.
  • Free delivery network across key townships.
  • Planned digital expansion for credit profiles and loyalty programs.

We prioritise ethical, compliant and community-first partners.

What we look for

We look for partners who are:

  • Serious about serving township communities long-term.
  • Transparent about pricing and terms.
  • Compliant with South African regulations (NCR, POPIA, tax, transport, etc.).
